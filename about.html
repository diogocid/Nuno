About Us

FOUNDER

Surfing is a big part of my life since I'm 14 years old, the age when I surfed my first wave and instantly fell in love with this sport.

It led me to explore the world. At a young age, I developed strong interest for traveling, in search of adventure, life experiences and new places to surf.

Having graduated from Aveiro University in Tourism Management, I decided to work in the travel and hospitality industry, and I occupied various positions in hotels, including management ones, in Lisbon, Portugal and Maputo, Mozambique.

After a few years, I was missing surfing a lot and I started realizing that life should be defined by the things you love doing, so I decided to dedicate my time to my passion and started to work as a surf instrutor full time.

With over 5 years of experience working with the best surfcamps in Europe, I decided to use my knowledge of surf spots in Portugal and my experience as a surf instructor to create something new, a concept that takes you away from the crowds, creating a unique experience of traveling, for both advanced surfers and beginners.
